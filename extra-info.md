## Ordered Lists
Ordered Lists automatically increment the number. Here's an example with code.

1. Apples
1. Oranges
1. Raspberry
1. Guava
1. Coconut

```
1. Apples
1. Oranges
1. Raspberry
1. Guava
1. Coconut
```

You can also nest the list. Indent the nested list items with atleast 2 spaces.

1. Apples
    1. Oranges
    1. Raspberry
    1. Guava
    1. Coconut
        1. Jelly
        1. Vanilla
        1. Mint
        1. Smoothie
    1. Strawberry
    1. Grapes
1. Banana
1. Peach

```
1. Apples
  1. Oranges
  1. Raspberry
  1. Guava
  1. Coconut
    1. Jelly
    1. Vanilla
    1. Mint
    1. Smoothie
  1. Strawberry
  1. Grapes
1. Banana
1. Peach
```
